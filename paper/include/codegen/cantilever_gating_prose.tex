\subsection{Cantilever-Gated Span Retrieval}
We compared control (no gating) and cantilever-gated arms across five prompts.
Control produced a mean of 23.40 tokens; gated produced 23.00.
Gating reduced bridge count (3 vs 3) and modulated return/loop coverage.
