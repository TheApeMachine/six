\subsection{U(1) Global Phase Rotation Symmetry}
We validated the $U(1)$ group action by verifying equivariance. Given a sequential rotation by
$\alpha=45.000^{\circ}$ and $\beta=90.000^{\circ}$, compared to a single rotation of
$\alpha+\beta=135.000^{\circ}$, the maximum deviation in retrieval scores was
$0.0000$, which is below the tolerance of $0.0000$.
Both operations successfully retrieved the exact same target coordinate structure.
